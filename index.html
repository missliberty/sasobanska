Hello

I am an initiative-grabbing individual based in Hong Kong, currently exploring the transformations in the business field, with a focus on startups and social media marketing and a self-taught UX/UI designer and event organiser.

  I believe in the power of technology to impact many spheres of life which has grown too inflexible.

  I experienced this firsthand as I helped to introduce medical software for doctors working in the slums of Cambodia, worked in a fintech startup to try and make the process of currency exchange cheaper and more convenient and during monthly coding meetings that I started in my university to encourage more people to create value with the power of their minds.

  I want to work on projects that make me excited to wake up everyday and that challenge me to grow my skills and shape my thoughts.

  Although I specialise in project management and marketing, I am a fast-learner and a seeker of new challenges and opportunities to learn.
